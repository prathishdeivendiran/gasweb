Why Mild Steel?

Why Infrastructure Projects Choose MS Pipeline

High Strength-to-Weight Ratio — Exceptional structural integrity for high-pressure applications.
Cost-Effective — Lower material and installation costs compared to stainless steel.
Excellent Weldability — Easy field fabrication and modification.
Temperature Resistant — Performs reliably from -29°C to 425°C.

MS vs Other Materials

Tensile Strength 410-550 MPa (MS) vs 200-300 MPa (others)
Cost Efficiency 30-40% lower than stainless steel
Durability 50+ years with proper coating
Installation Speed Fast welding & jointing
